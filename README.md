# Hey, I'm Mohamad 👋

**Front-end engineer with a generalist habit.**

By day I build Angular apps at [**A&G Insurance Australia (Budget Direct)**](https://www.budgetdirect.com.au) — quote and policy flows for ING, Virgin, Coles, and Ozicare across millions of Australians. By night I'm shipping a multi-tenant SaaS solo and running a used-car dealership in Brisbane. The framework on my CV says "Angular." What I actually do is solve business problems with whatever the problem needs.

Before Australia I rolled out an ERP for a 50-person engineering firm in Istanbul that was running on spreadsheets and goodwill. Before that I shipped Shopify storefronts for fragrance brands that smell better than my code — [Majouri](https://www.majouri.com/), [Maison Matine](https://maisonmatine.com), and the rest of the [MAA-ALTHAHAB](https://maa-althahab.com) family.

4 countries. 10+ projects shipped. 1 very opinionated `.eslintrc`.
