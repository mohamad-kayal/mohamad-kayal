**I turn messy business problems into practical systems people can actually use.**

By day I build Angular apps at [A&G Insurance Australia](https://www.budgetdirect.com.au) — car and home insurance quote & policy flows for Budget Direct, ING, Virgin, Coles, and Ozicare, serving millions of Australians. Outside of it I'm the Web and digital lead for [Brizcars](https://brizcars.com.au), a used-car dealership in Brisbane, on a website I wrote myself. It turns out a dealership is mostly a logistics-and-software problem in a hi-vis vest.

In between sat AutoInspectPro: a multi-tenant vehicle-inspection SaaS I designed, built, and ran solo — Postgres row-level security for tenant isolation, a BullMQ + Puppeteer PDF pipeline, a full production run on Fly.io with CI/CD, backups, and observability. It never found paying customers, so I sunset it in 2026 and kept the lessons. The build is the proof: [architecture write-up →](https://moekayal.me/autoinspectpro-architecture)

Before Australia: rolled out Odoo ERP for a 50-person engineering firm in Istanbul that had been running on spreadsheets and goodwill, shipped Shopify storefronts for fragrance brands that smell better than my code — [Majouri](https://www.majouri.com/), [Maison Matine](https://maisonmatine.com), the rest of the [MAA-ALTHAHAB](https://maa-althahab.com) family — and kept a law firm's IT alive in Abu Dhabi. Six years, four countries, one thread: understand how the business works, find the friction, ship the tool that makes it cleaner to run.
