# Hey, I'm Mohamad 👋

**Front-end engineer who makes `divs` behave and state management less painful.**

I build high-performance Angular applications at [**A&G Insurance Australia (Budget Direct)**](https://www.budgetdirect.com.au) in Brisbane — powering car and home insurance quote flows for millions of Australians through partnerships with ING, Virgin, Coles, and Ozicare.

Before Australia, I spent two years in Istanbul shipping Shopify storefronts for fragrance brands that smell better than my code — [Majouri](https://www.majouri.com/), [Maison Matine](https://maisonmatine.com), and the [MAA-ALTHAHAB](https://maa-althahab.com) family of labels.

3 countries. 10+ projects shipped. 1 very opinionated `.eslintrc`.
